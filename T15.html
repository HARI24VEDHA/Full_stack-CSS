CSS Transforms Showcase

Translate
Moves the element along the x and y axes.
Offsets the element's position.

Rotate
Rotates the element around its center.
Provides a rotating effect.

Scale
Resizes the element, making it larger or smaller.
Scales up or down.

Skew
Tilts the element along the x and y axes.
Creates a slanted effect.

Combines all transformations.
