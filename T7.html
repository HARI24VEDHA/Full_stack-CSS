Cybersecurity Overview

Key Concepts

Network Security: Protecting network infrastructure from unauthorized access and attacks.

Encryption: The process of encoding information to prevent unauthorized access.

Malware: Malicious software designed to disrupt, damage, or gain unauthorized access to systems.

Cybersecurity Measures

Effective cybersecurity measures are crucial for protecting sensitive data and maintaining the integrity of systems. Key strategies include:

  • Firewalls: Monitor and control incoming and outgoing network traffic.
  • Antivirus Software: Detect and remove malicious software.
  • Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS): Monitor networks for suspicious activities.
  • Regular Updates: Keep software and systems updated to patch vulnerabilities.

Implementing these measures helps safeguard against cyber threats and ensures a secure digital environment.
